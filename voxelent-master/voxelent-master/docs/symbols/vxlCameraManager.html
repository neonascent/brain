void remove(idx)
Removes a camera from the camera manager. Please notice that this operation will cause reindexing. So, if the camera manager has cameras 0, 1, 2 and camera 1 is removed then camera 2 will be now camera 1 so the index is maintained.

Parameters:

{Number} idx

the index of the camera to be removed
